About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to our Weather App — a project built to make weather forecasting accessible, accurate, and visually engaging. Inspired by retro aesthetics with modern technology, we provide real-time forecasts and alerts designed for everyday life.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to keep people safe and prepared. Whether you’re planning a trip, going to work, or watching for storms, our app gives you the latest weather updates, forecasts, and alerts right at your fingertips.

What We Offer

  • 🌤 Real-time weather updates
  • 📊 7-day forecast for better planning
  • ⚠️ Alerts & warnings for storms and heavy rainfall
  • 🌡️ Detailed data: temperature, humidity, wind, and more
  • 🗺 Location-based weather with reverse geocoding
